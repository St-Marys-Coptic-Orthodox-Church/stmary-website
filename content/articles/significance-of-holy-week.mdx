Holy Week is the most sacred period in the Coptic liturgical calendar, commemorating the Passion of our Lord Jesus Christ. Each day carries its own rich tradition and spiritual depth that has been preserved in the Coptic Church for nearly two millennia.

From Palm Sunday through Holy Saturday, the faithful gather daily for prayers, scripture readings, and liturgies that walk them step by step through the final days of our Lord's earthly life.
